Hello! I'm Stark the CEO of a Mostudio Photography

Hello, I'm Stark, a professional photographer based in Bangalore City. I specialize in capturing the beauty and diversity of urban life, from street scenes to architecture to portraits. I have been passionate about photography since I was a kid, and I love to explore new places and perspectives with my camera. Some of my achievements include winning the National Geographic Photo Contest in 2022, being featured in Time Magazine and The Bangaluru Times, and working with clients such as Nike, Apple, and Starbucks. I also enjoy teaching photography workshops and sharing my tips and tricks on my blog and YouTube channel. In my portfolio, you will find a selection of my best work, organized by categories such as cityscapes, people, and nature. You can also check out my testimonials, services, and contact information. I hope you enjoy browsing through my portfolio and feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or inquiries. Thank you for your interest in my photography.
